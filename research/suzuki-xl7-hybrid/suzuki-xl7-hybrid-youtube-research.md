## 2. Key Specification Data (dari Video Descriptions & Transcripts)

### 2.1 Transcript Insight: GridOto (fJUDA0xeORc)
GridOto review menggambarkan XL7 sebagai "salah satu mobil paling masuk akal di Indonesia":
- **Harga**: Flagship Alpha AT sekitar Rp 300 jutaan kecil, jauh di bawah BRV (Rp 370 juta) dan Xpander Cross (Rp 358-360 juta)
- **Akomodasi**: "Surprisingly lega" — baris kedua sliding, 14-15 jari legroom. Baris ketiga 4-5 jari (tinggi pengemudi 168cm). Masih masuk akal untuk dewasa.
- **Bagasi**: Dengan semua kursi aktif, masih cukup untuk 2 backpack + koper cabin size tegak. Kompartemen bawah luas.
- **Transmisi**: Torque converter AT dipuji — lebih awet dari CVT, terutama untuk karakter pengemudi Indonesia dan kontur geografis tidak flat.

## 5. Key Insight Summary

1. **XL7 Hybrid is the "most sensible car in Indonesia"** — GridOto's verdict echoed by many reviewers.
2. **AT transmission choice deliberately avoids CVT** — lauded for durability, especially in Indonesian driving conditions (hilly terrain, aggressive driving).
3. **Owner satisfaction is very high** — comments overwhelmingly positive, key praise: value, durability, fuel efficiency, cabin space.
4. **Mild hybrid effect is modest but real** — roughly 10-15% fuel savings. Not as dramatic as full hybrid. Some owners find it "nanggung" (half-hearted), others appreciate the simplicity.
5. **2 airbag limitation** is the most-cited safety weakness.
6. **Beta is the sweet spot** for many owners — cruise control, ESP, HHC, with significant savings over Alpha.
7. **Alpha's main upgrade is cosmetic** (E-Mirror touchscreen, two-tone, roof rail) — not major feature additions.
8. **Alpha Kuro is pure aesthetic** — 9 black accents, exclusive colors, AT-only.
