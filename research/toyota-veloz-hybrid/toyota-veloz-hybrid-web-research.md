## 7. Reviewer Opinions (Narrative)

### 7.1 Driving Comfort & NVH

Reviewers consistently praise the Veloz Hybrid's improved NVH and ride quality over the non-hybrid Veloz:

> "Suspensi lebih firm, stabil, tapi tetap nyaman. Kabin sangat senyap dan minim getaran — terutama di kecepatan rendah, mobil terasa glide seperti EV murni." — KumparanOto, First Drive

> "Toyota meningkatkan lapisan peredam di beberapa titik kritis — suara mesin, ban, dan angin jadi lebih senyap." — Teman-Mobil

Setting suspensi lebih firm dari non-hybrid membantu mengurangi limbung saat bermanuver atau membawa penumpang penuh. Bobot tambahan dari sistem hybrid juga menambah stabilitas.

### 7.2 Engine Performance & Hill Climb

> "Torsi instan dari motor listrik (141 Nm) menopang akselerasi bawah — mobil tidak terasa berat di tanjakan bahkan saat penuh muatan." — Kompas Otomotif, Quick Drive

> "Tarikan galak — akselerasi dari diam terasa responsif berkat motor listrik. Bukan sporty, tapi smooth dan bertenaga." — RM.id

Consensus: Hill climb performance is a strength due to instant electric torque. Not aggressive/sporty, but confident and smooth.

### 7.3 Interior & Practicality

> "Ruang kabin lega, ergonomis. Konfigurasi Long Sofa pada baris kedua nyaman untuk perjalanan keluarga." — Auto2000

> "Interior Modellista full black terasa lebih premium. Head unit 10 inci responsif dengan wireless AACP." — Avolta

> "Bagasi 207 liter cukup untuk harian, dan bisa diperbesar ke 1.160 liter dengan baris ketiga dilipat." — Liputan6

Reviewers note that row 3 space is typical for the segment — adequate for children or short trips, but tight for adults on long journeys.

### 7.4 Value for Money

> "MPV hybrid termurah di Indonesia. Harga entry Rp 303 juta (promo) membuat hybrid accessible untuk keluarga." — MaahadMuhammadi

> "Veloz Hybrid V HEV sudah cukup untuk kebutuhan harian — 60% pembeli memilih tipe ini." — Katadata

> "Selisih harga hybrid vs bensin terbayar dari penghematan BBM dalam 2-3 tahun pemakaian." — IDN Times

Reviewers generally consider V HEV the best value, while Q TSS Modellista is seen as a stretch unless TSS/ADAS is a must-have.

## 10. Key Findings Summary

### Strengths
1. **Fuel efficiency leader** — 22-26 km/l real-world, best in LMPV segment
2. **Smooth hybrid operation** — EV mode in city, instant torque for hills
3. **Competitive pricing** — Rp 303M (promo) makes it cheapest hybrid MPV in Indonesia
4. **Strong after-sales** — Toyota network, 8yr battery warranty, free 7x service
5. **Proven sales momentum** — 6,500+ orders in 4 months
6. **5-star ASEAN NCAP** — Structural safety proven
7. **70% resale guarantee** at year 3

### Weaknesses
1. **TSS/ADAS only on top variant** — Rp 385M+ to get safety tech
2. **V HEV stripped** — No kamera 360, wired AACP only, 9" head unit, 2 airbags
3. **Interior material quality** — Below some rival expectations
4. **Row 3 tight** — Typical of segment but not class-leading
5. **Indent 2-3 months** — High demand, limited supply
6. **No power sliding door** — Expected by some buyers in this segment
7. **Annual tax Rp 5.4-6.3M** — Not inexpensive

### Controversial Points
- Whether V HEV (60% of orders) is "enough" or Q HEV minimum for proper experience
- Whether Rp 82M gap between V HEV and Q TSS Modellista is justified
- Whether hybrid maintenance cost uncertainty outweighs fuel savings
