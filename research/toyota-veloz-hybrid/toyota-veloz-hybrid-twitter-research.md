## 6. Key Findings Summary

### Positive Consensus
- **Fuel efficiency is the #1 praised attribute** — real-world claims of 25-30 km/L in daily driving, with test results up to 35.5 km/L (GridOto eco-driving test)
- **Pricing widely regarded as aggressive and good value** — Rp 299-308 juta for a "real" full hybrid MPV is seen as a game-changer
- **"Real hybrid" distinction matters** — consumers actively differentiate Veloz's power-split hybrid from XL7's mild hybrid and appreciate the genuine technology
- **Toyota hybrid ecosystem trust** — existing infrastructure from Zenix, Yaris Cross, Camry provides peace of mind on service and parts
- **70% resale value guarantee at year 3** (Paket SmartDrive) removes risk perception for early adopters
- **6-year warranty** is frequently cited as a strong ownership benefit
- **Strong demand** — 6,500 inden units, 2-3 month waiting period, 900 units delivered by Auto2000 alone by March 2026
- **Self-charging system** praised for convenience (no plug-in needed)
- **TSS / ADAS on higher variants** adds real long-distance driving safety value, particularly Adaptive Cruise Control

### Negative Consensus
- **Annual tax ~Rp 5.5 juta** is consistently the top complaint — significantly higher than ICE Veloz and competitors like XL7
- **RON95 fuel requirement** adds running cost compared to competitors that accept Pertalite
- **Exterior "taxi" stigma persists** — at least some buyers feel the Avanza/Veloz lineage looks too much like a taxi, especially the rear
- **Battery replacement cost unknown** — brand new model means no long-term data yet
- **FWD limitation** noted for hilly terrain (Kalimantan, mountain roads) — ideal would be RWD hybrid

### Recurring Concerns
- **Pajak tahunan (annual tax):** Rp 5.5jt is a psychological barrier, especially for budget-conscious buyers who gravitate toward LMPV
- **BBM RON95:** Real-world compliance questioned — many Indonesian drivers prefer cheaper Pertalite
- **No dramatic improvement over previous Veloz** beyond the hybrid system — same platform, similar design
- **Top variant pricing (Q TSS Modellista at Rp 390jt)** encroaches into larger vehicle territory (Tiggo 8 CSH, pre-owned Zenix)
- **Selisih ~40jt vs bensin:** Payback period debated — makes more sense for 5+ year ownership and high daily mileage
