### 6.2 Value Rating by Variant

| Variant | Value Rating | Best For | Not For |
|---|---|---|---|
| **V HEV** (Rp 303-308M) | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Good | Budget-first buyers; fleet/commercial; pure fuel-savings seekers | Safety-conscious families (only 2 airbags); tech enthusiasts |
| **Q HEV** (Rp 325M) | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Best Value | Families wanting safety + convenience at lowest cost; 6 airbags + 360 cam + wireless AACP | Those who need ADAS/TSS |
| **Q Modellista** (Rp 350-354M) | ⭐⭐⭐ Niche | Style-conscious buyers who want RSE + aerokit aesthetic | Buyers who'd rather spend Rp 25M on ADAS; practical-first buyers |
| **Q TSS Modellista** (Rp 385-389M) | ⭐⭐⭐ Premium | Frequent toll/highway users who value ACC daily; tech-maximalist families | Budget-conscious buyers; city-only commuters (TSS less useful) |

### 6.3 Variant Recommendations

**Best Overall: Q HEV CVT (Rp 325.000.000)**
- The sweet spot. For Rp 17M more than V HEV, you get critical safety (6 airbags), convenience (kamera 360, wireless AACP), and comfort (wireless charger, ambient light) upgrades.
- YouTube reviewer consensus: Q HEV is the minimum recommended variant for a complete experience.

**Best Budget: V HEV CVT (Rp 303.000.000 promo)**
- The entry point that makes sense for pure efficiency seekers, fleet operators, or budget-first families.
- 60% of all orders — the people's choice.
- Compromise: 2 airbags is a real safety gap. Families with children should seriously consider the Q HEV.

**Best Premium: Q HEV TSS Modellista (Rp 385-389M)**
- Justified for buyers who do frequent long-distance toll driving where ACC and PCS add genuine daily safety value.
- Forced Modellista bundle is the trade-off — you pay for the aerokit whether you want it or not.
- Warning: At Rp 385M+, you're approaching Chery Tiggo 8 CSH (larger SUV) and pre-owned Innova Zenix Hybrid territory.

**Skip Unless You Love The Look: Q Modellista (Rp 350-354M)**
- The weakest value proposition. Rp 25M over Q HEV for what is essentially cosmetic (aerokit, dual-tone velg, LED fog lamp) + RSE.
- If you'd rather have ADAS than looks, save Rp 25M here and add Rp 10M to jump to TSS.
- If you don't need RSE or aerokit, stay at Q HEV and save Rp 25M.
