### What does each price step add?

**Cartenz Trend MT → Trend IVT (+Rp 10.0 juta)**
- IVT automatic transmission (replaces 6MT)
- ✅ Recommended unless you specifically want manual

**Cartenz Trend IVT → Style IVT (+Rp 20.0 juta)**
- Wireless CarPlay/Android Auto
- Smart Key + Push Start
- ESC + VSM
- 10.25" head unit (from 8")
- Front parking sensors (adds to existing rear)
- SmartSense ADAS (radar-based): FCA, LKA/LFA, BCA, RCCA, DAW
- ✅ **BIGGEST value jump** — ADAS alone is worth the upgrade

**Cartenz Style IVT → Smart HSS (+Rp 33.0 juta)**
- SCC with Stop & Go (adaptive cruise with full-stop capability)
- 10.25" digital cluster (from 4.2" TFT)
- EPB + Auto Hold
- Built-in navigation
- Wireless charger
- Ambient lighting
- Seatback tables (row 2)
- 6 airbags (from 2)
- ⚠️ Large price jump but adds significant safety (airbags) and convenience

**Cartenz Smart HSS → Prime HSS (+Rp 17.5 juta)**
- BVM (Blind-spot View Monitor — camera on instrument cluster)
- SVM (Surround View Monitor / 360° camera)
- ✅ Premium features at moderate premium

**Cartenz Prime HSS → Cartenz X Trend (+Rp 11.5 juta)**
- Crossover body styling (cladding, roof rails, wider body)
- +35mm length, +40mm width, +45mm height
- Ground clearance: 190–205mm (from 185mm)
- 215/55 R17 wheels (from 195/60 R16)
- Shift-by-wire transmission
- Paddle shifters
- 10.25" digital cluster
- Smart Key + Push Start (standard)
- EPB + Auto Hold
- SCC with Stop & Go
- Ambient lighting
- Built-in navigation
- ⚠️ BUT loses BVM, SVM, 6 airbags (only 2), wireless charger, seatback tables
- ⚠️ **Trade-off**: Crossover styling + driving features vs safety/convenience features

**Cartenz X Trend → Cartenz X Style (+Rp 13.0 juta)**
- BVM (Blind-spot View Monitor)
- 6 airbags (from 2)
- Wireless charger
- ✅ Critical safety upgrade (airbags jump from 2 to 6)

**Cartenz X Style → Cartenz X Prime Package (+Rp 17.0 juta)**
- SVM (360° camera / Surround View Monitor)
- BOSE 8-speaker audio
- Ventilated driver seat
- ✅ Premium luxury features — nice-to-have, not essential

## Part 5: Variant Recommendation Summary

### For budget-conscious buyers (< Rp 300 juta):
→ **Cartenz Style IVT (Rp 299.9 juta)**
- Gets ADAS, wireless connectivity, ESC, 10.25" head unit
- ⚠️ Only 2 airbags — the biggest compromise
- Still safer than most competitors at this price

### For safety-focused family buyers:
→ **Cartenz Smart HSS (Rp 332.9 juta)** ⭐ RECOMMENDED
- Gets 6 airbags + full SmartSense + SCC Stop&Go
- Best balance of safety, features, and price
- Missing: BVM, SVM (nice-to-have, not essential)

### For feature-maximizers staying with MPV body:
→ **Cartenz Prime HSS (Rp 350.4 juta)** ⭐ BEST VALUE
- Everything in Smart HSS plus BVM and SVM (360° camera)
- Highest value index across all variants
- Premium without crossover premium pricing

### For crossover/LSUV enthusiasts:
→ **Cartenz X Style (Rp 374.9 juta)**
- Gets crossover styling, shift-by-wire, paddle shifters, higher ground clearance
- 6 airbags, BVM, wireless charger
- Best balance within Cartenz X lineup

### For maximum everything:
→ **Cartenz X Prime Package (Rp 391.9 juta)**
- Complete feature set including BOSE, ventilated seat, SVM
- "Mini-Palisade" experience
- Premium price but tangible premium experience

## Part 6: Cartenz vs Cartenz X — Should You Pay the Crossover Premium?

### Price Premium: Rp 11.5–41.5 juta more for Cartenz X

### What you gain with Cartenz X:
✅ Crossover body styling (cladding, wider stance, roof rails)
✅ Ground clearance: 190–205mm (vs 185mm)
✅ 215/55 R17 wheels (better grip, more road presence)
✅ Shift-by-wire transmission
✅ Paddle shifters
✅ BOSE audio (Prime Package only)
✅ Ventilated driver seat (Prime Package only)

### What you might lose (comparing same-tier):
⚠️ Cartenz X Trend has only 2 airbags (Cartenz Smart HSS has 6)
⚠️ Cartenz X Trend lacks wireless charger (Cartenz Smart HSS has it)
⚠️ Cartenz X Trend lacks seatback tables
⚠️ Ride may be slightly firmer on 215/55 R17 tires

### Verdict:
- **If comfort and safety are priority** → Cartenz Smart HSS or Prime HSS offers more safety features per rupiah
- **If styling, ground clearance, and driving engagement are priority** → Cartenz X delivers genuine crossover experience
- **Cross-tier comparison trap**: Cartenz X Trend (Rp 361.9 juta) has FEWER safety features than Cartenz Smart HSS (Rp 332.9 juta) despite costing Rp 29 juta more. Always compare features, not just model names
