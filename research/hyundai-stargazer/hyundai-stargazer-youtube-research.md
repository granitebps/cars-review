## Key Findings from YouTube Reviews

### 1. Design & Exterior

**Original Stargazer (2022)**:
- Polarizing front-end design — "love it or hate it" reaction consistent across most reviewers
- Rear design generally praised as cleaner than front
- YouTube comment: "XL7 lupa ngedesign yg belakang, Stargazer lupa ngedesign yg depan" — a recurring sentiment
- Om Mobi noted the design grows on you over time
- OtoDriver (Fitra Eri) acknowledged futuristic appeal but noted it wouldn't appeal to all

**Stargazer X (2023)**:
- Reviewers agreed the body cladding and raised stance improved rugged appeal
- Ridwan Hanif: "LEBIH MAHAL 25 JUTA" — questioned whether the X premium was justified
- GridOto: compared favorably against Xpander Cross in terms of features per rupiah
- Moladin hill-climb test (Jogja): Stargazer X with 8 passengers completed extreme incline — demonstrated good hill-climb capability

**Stargazer Cartenz (2025)**:
- Universal praise for Palisade-inspired front design — "facelift rasa all-new" (multiple reviewers)
- Om Mobi: "BERUBAH BANYAK" — noted dashboard, infotainment, and overall interior quality significantly upgraded
- Carmudi Indonesia: "MIMPI BURUK VELOZ dan XPANDER" — highlighted feature advantage over competitors
- KOKOMOBIL: Cangar hill-climb test completed successfully — extreme mountain road conditions, rain, load
- B Channel: Detailed in-depth tour of Trend variant — showed even entry variant got meaningful upgrades
- Teguhku: Style vs Smart HSS comparison showed Smart HSS is the sweet spot for ADAS seekers
- YouTube comments overwhelmingly positive on interior quality and design improvement

**Stargazer Cartenz X (2025)**:
- Ridwan Hanif: "LSUV TERBAIK DAN TERMAHAL!" — praised completeness of features but noted price premium
- Jason Nathaniel: Questioned whether the premium pricing was worth it compared to Cartenz
- Carmudi: Extensive comparison with Xpander Cross and Rush — Cartenz X won on features and refinement
- Otohubdotco: "Serba Ada" — noted it outclasses Xpander Cross, XL7, and BR-V on features

### 2. Interior Quality

**Common across all variants:**
- YouTube reviewers consistently rank Stargazer interior above Xpander and Veloz in material quality
- Captain seat option (baris ke-2) praised for comfort and premium feel
- 3rd row usable for short/medium trips but not great for long journeys (legs up, thin cushion)

**Cartenz/Cartenz X improvements:**
- Dashboard redesigned with 10.25" dual screens — "layaknya mobil premium" (Carmudi)
- Shift-by-wire transmission (Cartenz X) — "mirip Palisade" (LugNutz, HeiDude comment)
- Om Mobi: Interior upgraded quality is the standout improvement from previous generation
- Kacamata Andi Pras: Detailed unboxing showed high-quality materials, soft-touch on key surfaces
- YouTube comment @A.massaro: "bagian depan itu amazing sih, boleh dibilang paling bagus di kelasnya" (48 likes)
- YouTube comment @Skipr23: Listed upgrades — 360 Camera, ADAS radar, ventilated seat, real roof rail, head unit improved

### 3. Driving Impressions & Comfort

**Engine:**
- 1.5L Kappa NA engine: 115 PS / 144 Nm — adequate but not outstanding
- Reviewers agree: sufficient for city/highway, adequate for hills with full load
- OtoDriver: fuel consumption is competitive — "mind blowing" fuel efficiency per Stargazer 2022 review
- IVT (chain-type CVT): smoother than typical belt-CVT but some noise at high RPM
- Dokter Mobil Indonesia: IVT explained as chain-based — more responsive than belt CVT, less rubber band

**Comfort:**
- Suspension consistently praised as one of the most comfortable in LMPV class
- KOKOMOBIL Cangar road trip: comfortable over long distances, even on poor roads
- MacPherson strut + CTBA — balanced setup, neither too soft nor too firm
- Stargazer X: slightly firmer due to 205/50 R17 tires vs 195/60 R16 on regular Stargazer
- Cartenz maintains similar comfort character; Cartenz X slightly firmer with larger wheels
- @drosandi8912 (Stargazer X owner, YouTube comment): "dari sisi kenyamanan & fitur safetynya sy berasa bawa mobil yg kelasnya diatas R3"

**Hill-Climb Capability:**
- Moladin: Stargazer X with 8 people completed dangerous climb in Jogja — strong performance
- KOKOMOBIL: Cartenz tested on Cangar pass (extreme mountain road in East Java) — completed with rain and load
- Multiple comparison videos show Stargazer/Cartenz handles inclines better than Veloz/Avanza FWD
- YouTube comment @leinadyon: "Veloz buat tanjakan di Trawas cuma 5 orang tanpa muatan aja udah ngos-ngosan klepek"
- YouTube comment @jerijelajahriders875: "Alesan ane ngambil stargazer gara2 moladin test nanjak"

**NVH:**
- Engine noise noticeable at high RPM, particularly with IVT under load
- Wind noise acceptable for class
- Road noise moderate — better than Xpander but not class-leading
- Cartenz X with larger wheels may increase road noise slightly
- Interior sound insulation better than most LMPV rivals according to multiple reviewers

### 4. Safety & ADAS (SmartSense)

**Original Stargazer:**
- SmartSense ADAS only available on Prime variant
- Driving Indonesia POV: SmartSense LFA + SCC works well, "SELF DRIVING!?" title captures the surprise
- Camera-based system on original model

**Cartenz/Cartenz X:**
- Major upgrade to radar-based SmartSense
- Available from Style variant onwards (Cartenz) and all variants (Cartenz X)
- Features include: SCC with Stop & Go, LFA (Lane Following Assist), FCA (Forward Collision Avoidance), BCA (Blind-spot Collision Avoidance), BVM (Blind-spot View Monitor), SVM (Surround View Monitor)
- Carmudi: "sudah ada adaptive cruise control dan lane centering, lepas gas, rem, bahkan tangan setir sesaat tetap aman"
- YouTube comment @RobRichRoy: "Weh weh weh 360, spedometer full digital, ADAS stop n go.. SADIS TENAN INI" (40 likes)

### 6. Competitor Comparisons (Reviewer Consensus)

**Stargazer vs Xpander:**
- OtoDriver (1.4M views comparison): Stargazer wins on comfort, features, fuel efficiency; Xpander wins on space, resale, brand trust
- Most reviewers agree Stargazer offers more value per rupiah but Xpander has stronger dealer network and resale

**Stargazer X vs Xpander Cross:**
- GridOto: Stargazer X is "lebih mantap" overall with better features
- Moladin: Xpander Cross has better brand recognition but Stargazer X offers more per rupiah
- OTW Indonesia: Stargazer X wins on features/comfort, Xpander Cross on resale/brand familiarity

**Cartenz X vs Veloz vs Destinator:**
- Moladin mega-comparison: Cartenz X best value on features, Veloz best on fuel economy (hybrid), Destinator best on power/space
- LugNutz (975K views): Cartenz X "memudahkan hidup" most with tech features
- Multiple comments requesting Veloz Hybrid hill-climb test suggests Veloz perceived as weak on inclines
- YouTube comment @distyland5972 (155 likes): "Kirain bakal veloz hybrid yg di test. Penasaran aja, soalnya berkali-kali veloz/avanza FWD gagal nanjak test mulu"

### 7. Market Sentiment from YouTube Comments

**Overall Sentiment: Strongly Positive for Cartenz/Cartenz X**

Key themes:
1. **"Facelift rasa All-New"** — most common compliment; viewers see Cartenz as essentially a new car
2. **Interior quality praise** — "keterlaluan" (excessive), "amazing", "paling bagus di kelasnya"
3. **Feature advantage** — viewers recognize Hyundai packs more features than Japanese competitors at similar price
4. **Design improvement** — universally praised; old Stargazer front was controversial, new Cartenz front well-received
5. **Concern about brand trust** — some commenters still hesitant about Korean brand reliability/dealer network
6. **Spare parts fear** — recurring concern that Hyundai may exit Indonesia or parts availability may be poor long-term
7. **Price appreciation** — many viewers find Cartenz X expensive but acknowledge the value proposition
8. **Hill-climb confidence** — Stargazer hill-climb videos have driven actual purchase decisions
