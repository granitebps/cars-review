## Key Themes & Sentiment Analysis

### 1. Overall Sentiment

**Positive sentiment dominates** across Twitter/X discussions:
- Comfort, features, and value-for-money are the most praised aspects
- Design of Cartenz/Cartenz X well-received (big improvement from original Stargazer)
- SmartSense ADAS frequently highlighted as differentiator in the LMPV class
- Several verified accounts and influencers publicly endorse Stargazer

**Negative/cautionary sentiment centers on:**
- Radiator fan recall (gen-1 Stargazer/Creta pre-2024)
- Dealer network concerns (limited coverage outside major cities)
- Brand trust / resale value anxiety
- Some battery drain issues reportedly linked to Bluelink connectivity

### 7. Dealer Network & Aftersales Concerns

- @ceh_fajar: 3-year owner in daerah (region) — no complaints about car, but "agak terkucilkan" with limited showrooms, workshops, promos outside major cities
- @eRaptorrr: Critical of Hyundai management direction since TAM CFO joined — "gk akan mereka keluarin sistem sewa kalo jualan unit laku" (referring to Hyundai Subscribe program)
- Multiple dealer accounts pushing Cartenz/Cartenz X aggressively with cashback offers
- Hyundai Subscribe service (rental model) now includes Stargazer Cartenz — seen as alternative to buying

### 8. Resale Value & Market Perception

- Verified accounts note significant depreciation for gen-1 Stargazer (2022 units now Rp 175-210 juta from Rp 243-321 juta new)
- @ardiindra: Compared Cartenz X and Veloz Hybrid resale — "harga jual kembali sama lah, krna hybrid pasti ada umur baterai"
- Hyundai's 70% resale value guarantee (3-year trade-in) frequently mentioned as mitigating factor
- Brand trust still lower than Toyota/Mitsubishi for resale

### 9. International Market Expansion

- Multiple Malaysian automotive accounts noting Stargazer Cartenz coming to Malaysia Q4 2026
- @paultan (Malaysia): "Hyundai Stargazer, MPV segmen-B baru yang akan datang ke Malaysia hujung tahun ini!"
