## 7. Common Problems & Known Issues

### 1. Radiator Fan Recall (Critical — Gen-1 Only)
- **Affected**: Stargazer & Creta produced before 2024
- **Issue**: Radiator fan motor failure causing overheating
- **Consequence**: Engine damage if not caught early
- **Status**: Official recall conducted; free replacement at Hyundai dealers
- **Recommendation**: Check recall status before buying used gen-1 Stargazer

### 2. Bluelink Battery Drain
- **Issue**: Bluelink connectivity feature reported to drain 12V battery
- **Symptom**: Difficulty starting after vehicle sits for extended period
- **Frequency**: Occasional — reported by multiple owners on forums and Twitter
- **Workaround**: Disable Bluelink when parking for extended periods

### 3. IVT Noise at High RPM
- **Issue**: Chain-based IVT transmits more noise than expected under hard acceleration
- **Context**: IVT uses chain (not belt) — more durable but noisier than belt CVT at high load
- **Severity**: Cosmetic/NVH annoyance, not mechanical failure
- **Note**: Improved in Cartenz models with better sound insulation

### 4. Wireless CarPlay/Android Auto Disconnection
- **Issue**: Bluetooth connection drops between phone and head unit
- **Root Cause**: Default Bluetooth name "STARGAZER" on all units — causes interference when multiple Stargazers are nearby
- **Fix**: Rename Bluetooth device name in head unit settings

### 5. 3rd Row Comfort Limitations
- **Issue**: 3rd row seats have minimal legroom, thin cushion
- **Context**: Common across all LMPV class vehicles
- **Severity**: Acceptable for short trips; uncomfortable for long journeys
- **Note**: Captain seat option reduces 3rd row access

### 6. Design Polarization (Gen-1 Only)
- **Issue**: Front-end design on original Stargazer was controversial
- **Resolution**: Completely redesigned for Cartenz — universally well-received
- **Impact on resale**: May have contributed to faster depreciation of gen-1

### Key Competitive Advantages
1. **Most complete ADAS** in LMPV class (radar-based SmartSense with SCC Stop&Go)
2. **Best interior quality** at price point — widely acknowledged by reviewers
3. **Strong hill-climb capability** — outperforms Veloz/Avanza in incline tests
4. **IVT chain transmission** — more responsive than typical belt CVT
5. **Free Service 5yr/75k km** — strongest aftersales package in class
6. **70% resale guarantee** — addresses biggest buyer concern

### Key Competitive Disadvantages
1. **Dealer network smaller** than Toyota/Mitsubishi/Honda
2. **Brand trust lower** — especially outside Java
3. **Resale value weaker** than Toyota equivalents
4. **Ground clearance decreased** on Cartenz (185mm) vs original Stargazer (195mm)
5. **No hybrid option** — Toyota Veloz Hybrid offers better fuel economy
6. **Engine power** — adequate but not class-leading (Honda BR-V has 121 PS)
