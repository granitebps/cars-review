### From Otodriver Review (mC6gmaCOYwE) — Comment Summary by @yohanest3890 (222 likes)

**Kelebihan (Strengths):**

- Best engine, acceleration 0-100 in 11.6s — best in LSUV and LMPV class.
- Suspension as mature as Xpander Cross (not as soft as regular Xpander, but more composed with better handling).
- Safety features arguably best in class.
- Handling mature and best in class.
- Has auto high beam and brake overriding system (not found in competitors).
- 6 airbags (HS variant).
- Timeless design.
- Remote engine start/stop.
- Walk-away auto lock.

**Kekurangan (Weaknesses):**

- Sound insulation improved but still not "kedap" — especially during rain.
- Manual parking brake.
- No rear defogger.
- No telescopic steering.

**Fitra Eri's conclusion:** "Performance, suspension, and safety features are THE BEST in the class right now."

## 4. REVIEWER CONSENSUS

### Strengths (agreed by 3+ reviewers)

1. **Best engine in class** — 121 PS, 0-100 in 10.9–11.6s, consistently praised across all major reviews.
2. **Best handling/driving dynamics** — fun to drive, stable cornering, mature ride feel.
3. **Strong hill climb** — passed 17° fully loaded (GridOto); owners independently confirm real-world climbing ability.
4. **Honda Sensing ADAS** — real safety benefit; CMBS credited with saving lives in owner testimonials.
5. **Design/timeless styling** — universally praised as attractive, modern, and not easily outdated.
6. **Walk-away auto lock & remote start** — practical convenience features appreciated by owners and reviewers alike.

### Weaknesses (agreed by 3+ reviewers)

1. **Price/overprice** — most expensive in class; repeatedly noted by reviewers and commenters.
2. **NVH/cabin noise** — rain noise, wind noise, tire noise at speed; improved over gen-1 but still not "kedap."
3. **No telescopic steering** — every major reviewer mentions this omission.
4. **No rear defogger** — Fitra Eri specifically called this out as feeling "cheap."
5. **Manual parking brake** — no EPB on any variant; widely criticized.
6. **Rear drum brakes** — not 4-disc like some competitors.
7. **Heavy tailgate** — hard to close; won't latch with a light push.

### Divided Opinions

- **CVT feel**: Some praise smooth delivery; others note "ngeden" (straining) under hard acceleration.
- **Suspension**: Generally praised as balanced, but some find it too firm over rough roads.
- **Interior quality**: Leather looks good but feels synthetic/slippery; plastics acceptable but not premium.
